Built for the students the system left behind Most counselling psychology students in India face the same paradox: you cannot become a practitioner without practice, but practice is locked behind structures that are rigid, geographically limited, and often financially out of reach. NurtureNest was built to change that. We offer modular, online internship experiences — each one paired with a licensed supervisor — designed for students in Year 1 through postgraduate programmes, wherever they are in India. Our mission is simple: accessible, flexible, and ethically supervised training that equips the next generation of therapists with confidence, competence, and ethical grounding — not just hours on a certificate. ⚖️ Ethical Practice All supervision follows RCI/APA professional codes of conduct. 🌍 Accessibility Designed for Tier 1, 2, and 3 cities. Cost is never a barrier. 🎯 Skill Development Every module maps to measurable counselling competencies. 🔒 Confidentiality Strict privacy for students and clients. Training built-in from Day 1. 🌱 Student Empowerment Self-directed learning with professional scaffolding and real feedback. 📋 Certification Each completed module issues a NurtureNest Completion Certificate. DAY 1–2 Empathic Listening Read the assigned chapter on empathic listening. Then reflect for 30 minutes: write about a moment you truly felt heard — what made it meaningful, and what it felt like. 📝 Journal entry — min. 300 words DAY 3 Active Listening Roleplay Conduct a 15-minute mock conversation with a peer or family member. Record it on your phone. Self-review using the Active Listening Checklist — 8 observable behaviours. 🎙 Recording + completed checklist DAY 4–5 Non-Verbal Communication Watch a provided therapy video.
