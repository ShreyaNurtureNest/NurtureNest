What is NurtureNest?

Built for the students
the system left behind

Most counselling psychology students in India face the same paradox: you cannot become a practitioner without practice, but practice is locked behind structures that are rigid, geographically limited, and often financially out of reach.

NurtureNest was built to change that. We offer modular, online internship experiences — each one paired with a licensed supervisor — designed for students in Year 1 through postgraduate programmes, wherever they are in India.

Our mission is simple: accessible, flexible, and ethically supervised training that equips the next generation of therapists with confidence, competence, and ethical grounding — not just hours on a certificate.

Inside Week 1

What you'll do each day

Every activity is designed to build a specific, measurable counselling skill — with a clear deliverable so you can see your own progress.

Day 1–2

Empathic Listening

Read the assigned chapter on empathic listening. Then reflect for 30 minutes: write about a moment you truly felt heard — what made it meaningful, and what it felt like.

📝 Journal entry — min. 300 words
Day 3

Active Listening Roleplay

Conduct a 15-minute mock conversation with a peer or family member. Record it on your phone. Self-review using the Active Listening Checklist — 8 observable behaviours.

🎙 Recording + completed checklist
Day 4–5

Non-Verbal Communication

Watch a provided therapy video. Complete the Video Analysis Worksheet — identify 5 non-verbal behaviours you observe and describe their likely impact on the therapeutic relationship.

📋 Completed analysis worksheet
Day 6–7

Ethics in Counselling

Read a real-world ethics case vignette and write a 250-word response addressing the ethical principles in conflict, your responsibilities, and what you would do. Share in the group forum.

💬 Forum post + written response
End of Week

Supervisor Check-In

A 30-minute 1:1 video call with your assigned licensed supervisor. Review your journal, checklist, and reflections together. Receive personalised feedback on your Week 1 practice.
